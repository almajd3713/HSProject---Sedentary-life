Sedentary Life
Definitions and Solutions

The Definition

A Sedentary lifestyle is a lifestyle that involves little to no physical activities, and it's mostly described by sitting and spending most of the time using electronic devices, and the lack of physical exercise

And it's more common than what do you think !
And of course, it's not healthy at all ! It causes a lot of diseases, including:

Cardiovascular Disease

Type 2 Diabetes

and not only that ! the harm even reaches the mental side of our health !

Chronic Sterss

Depression

Now that it's clear how harmful this lifestyle is, what benefits could phyiscal activity bring for us ?

Confidence

Doing physical activity tends to improve our body's image. This is a very big factor for self-confidence, as most people tend not to be confident when their image isn't as they like it to.

Leadership

People who do physical exercise tend to do better when handling leadership positions, as more stress that such positions cause would dissipate with these activities. It also allows for creativity to flourish, which is a requirement for being a leader.

Happiness

The American Psychiatric Association confirmed that physical activity is an effective treatment for depression. It also produces the "happiness" hormones, which lifts up the mood and makes the person happy.
